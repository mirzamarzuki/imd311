My Friend

"Sisters at heart and partners in crime. It's the connection we can't explain."

Friendship is a very important relationship in life. It happens between two or more people not related by blood. People who indulge in friendship are called friends. Friends are always there for you whenever you need help. Without friendship, we will be lonely. It fills the loneliness and makes life worth. Friendship can form between anyone.
